<Callout>
Non-sedating antihistamine.
Used for allergic rhinitis and chronic urticaria.
Long-acting metabolite of loratadine.
</Callout>

---

## Indication

- Allergic rhinitis (seasonal and perennial)
- Chronic idiopathic urticaria
- Itchy skin or sneezing due to allergy

---

## Administration

- Can be taken with or without food.
- Usually taken in the morning.
- Swallow tablet whole or use oral syrup as prescribed.

---

## Contraindications

- Hypersensitivity to desloratadine or loratadine
- Hereditary fructose intolerance (for syrup form)

---

## Adverse Reactions

- **Common**
  - Headache
  - Fatigue
  - Dry mouth

---

## Pregnancy and Lactation

<Callout>
**Pregnancy:**
Limited human data. Animal studies show no harm. Use if benefits outweigh risks.

**Lactation:**
Unknown if excreted in human milk. Use with caution.
</Callout>
