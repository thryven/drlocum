# Cephalexin

## Dosing

- **Standard dose:** 12.5 mg/kg twice daily (BD).
- **Maximum dose:** 500 mg per dose.

## High-level summary

Cephalexin is a cephalosporin antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.

## Indications

- Respiratory tract infections
- Otitis media (ear infections)
- Skin and soft tissue infections
- Bone infections
- Genitourinary tract infections

## Contraindications

- Known allergy to cephalosporins.
- Use with caution in patients with a history of penicillin allergy due to potential cross-reactivity.

## Adverse effects

- Gastrointestinal upset (diarrhea, nausea, vomiting)
- Rash
- Headache

## Pregnancy & Lactation

- Generally considered safe during pregnancy.
- Excreted in breast milk in small amounts; use with caution.
