## Indication

- Respiratory, ENT, skin and soft tissue,

---

## Dose

- Adults: 500/125 mg three times daily or 875/125 mg twice daily
- Paediatrics: 25â€“45 mg/kg/day divided

---

## Administration

- Take with food to improve absorption and reduce gastrointestinal upset.

---

## Contraindications

<Alert type="error">
  - Hypersensitivity to penicillins or clavulanic acid.
</Alert>

---

## Adverse Reactions

- Gastrointestinal upset (nausea, diarrhoea) and rash are common. Clostridioides difficile infection is a potential risk.

---

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Generally acceptable if indicated; follow local guidance and product SmPC.
