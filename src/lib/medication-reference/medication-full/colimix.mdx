<Callout>
  Colimix is a tradename for antispasmodic preparations used in colic and GI
  spasm. Formulations may vary by region; consult the local product label for
  precise composition and dosing.
</Callout>

---

## Indication

- Symptomatic relief of abdominal cramping and colic.

---

## Dose

- Follow the product-specific dosing instructions. When product details are unknown, consult pharmacy or the product SmPC.

---

## Administration

- Administer as directed by product labelling.

---

## Contraindications

<Alert type="error">
  - Hypersensitivity to any component of the formulation.
</Alert>

---

## Adverse Reactions

- Varies with formulation; may include anticholinergic effects such as dry mouth and blurred vision.

---

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Consult local guidance and the product SmPC.
