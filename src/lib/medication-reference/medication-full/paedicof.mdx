<Callout>
  Paedicof is a tradename commonly used for paediatric cough/expectorant syrups.
  Formulations vary; check the product label for active ingredients and dosing.
</Callout>

---

## Indication

- Symptomatic relief of cough and mucus in children when appropriate.

---

## Dose

- Follow the product label for age/weight-specific dosing. If product information is unavailable, consult a pharmacist or paediatrician.

---

## Administration

- Do not exceed recommended doses. Avoid combination products containing multiple sedating agents in young children.

---

## Contraindications

<Alert type="error">
  - Hypersensitivity to any of the formulation components.
</Alert>

---

## Adverse Reactions

- Varies with formulation; possible drowsiness, GI upset, or rare allergic reactions.

---

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Product-dependent; consult product SmPC and specialist advice for use in breastfeeding mothers.
