<Callout>
  Hyoscine butylbromide is an antispasmodic used to relieve smooth muscle spasm
  in the gastrointestinal tract.
</Callout>

## Indication

- Relief of abdominal cramps and spasms, colic, and other spasm-related discomfort.

## Dose

- Adults: commonly 10â€“20 mg orally three times a day as needed; follow product labelling for specific formulations.
- Paediatrics: use product-specific guidance or specialist advice.

## Administration

- May be taken with or without food depending on product instructions.

## Contraindications

<Alert type="error">
  - Known hypersensitivity to hyoscine or anticholinergics. - Conditions where
  anticholinergic effects are problematic (e.g., acute glaucoma, severe
  prostatic hypertrophy, paralytic ileus).
</Alert>

## Adverse Reactions

- Dry mouth, blurred vision, constipation, urinary retention, tachycardia.

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Use only if clearly needed
