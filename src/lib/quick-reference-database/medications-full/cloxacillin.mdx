<Callout>
  Cloxacillin is a penicillinase-resistant penicillin primarily used for
  penicillinase-producing staphylococcal infections (skin, bone, and soft tissue
  infections).
</Callout>

---

## Indication

- Staphylococcal infections where penicillinase resistance is required (e.g., skin and soft tissue infections, osteomyelitis).

---

---

## Administration

- Take on an empty stomach if possible (oral formulations), unless formulation or tolerance indicates otherwise.

---

## Contraindications

<Alert type="error">- Known hypersensitivity to penicillins.</Alert>

---

## Adverse Reactions

- Gastrointestinal upset, hypersensitivity reactions (rash, anaphylaxis), hepatic enzyme abnormalities (rare).

---

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Use when clinically indicated; consult product information.
